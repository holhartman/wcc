Dr. Sandra Orsulic's research explores the impact of cellular environments on the growth and spread of ovarian cancer.

Interesting Facts

  1. Dr. Orsulic is currently the director of research for women's cancer biology at Cedars-Sinai Medical Center.
  2. She earned a bachelor's degree in mathematics from the University of Zagreb.
  3. Today she conducts much of her cancer research using genetically engineered mice.
  4. One of her areas of interest is developing pre-clinical models for targeted cancer treatment.
